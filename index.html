About Me

I am Fumiko Horan, a professional kimono stylist born in Taiwan, proficient in Chinese, Japanese, and English languages. I resided in Japan from 1980 to 2000 and immigrated to Canada in 2001.

In 2019, I joined the Costume Department of the Vancouver Film Industry union, where my primary role involves dressing lead actors in kimono attire for film productions. Notably, I took on a leadership role in the kimono styling team during the filming of the 2022 FX online movie "ShoGun."

Resume

Currently serving as a kimono-wearing instructor at the Vancouver Japanese Cultural Centre, providing guidance on traditional Japanese handmade flower ornament crafts.

Currently instructing kimono wearing at the "Neighbor Group" social organization in Vancouver.

Currently serving as the team leader for kimono attire in the Costume Department of the Vancouver Film Industry union IATSE Local 891.

Planning the kimono showcase for the Vancouver International Fashion Show.

Participating in the Vancouver VanDu San Garden Cherry Blossom Festival as a kimono-wearing activity coordinator.
